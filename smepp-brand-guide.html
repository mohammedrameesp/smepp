7Logo Usage Rules

Do's and don'ts for logo application

‚úì Do

  • Use approved colors only
  • Maintain clear space around logo
  • Use on white or light backgrounds
  • Use icon version for small sizes
  • Keep the "++" with "SME"

‚úó Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the colors
  • Add effects (shadows, glows)
  • Rotate the logo
  • Use on busy backgrounds
  • Separate "SME" from "++"
